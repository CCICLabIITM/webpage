Welcome to CCIC Lab

Welcome to the Computational Catalysis and Interfacial Chemistry (CCIC) research group, led by Dr. Sooraj Kunnikuruvan. The group focuses on the computational discovery of catalysts and the mechanistic understanding of chemical reactions at interfaces, addressing challenges in energy, sustainability, and advanced functional materials.
